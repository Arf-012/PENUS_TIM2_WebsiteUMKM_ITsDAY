About

Founded in 2019, Suka-Suka Chicken is a leading industry standard in Fried Chicken technology. Combining handmade quality, with efficiency in every piece of fried deliciousness. Founded by a mother and her son, Suka-Suka Chicken values classic family tradition, along with all-around good food.

Main Course

Paket Lengkap Crispy Chicken

This crispy chicken is seasoned with our secret blend of spices and cooked to golden perfection. Each bite offers an irresistible crunch and juicy tenderness inside. A classic favorite!

Paket Ayam Pedas Nikmat

For those who crave a fiery kick, our spicy hot chicken is marinated in a special blend of chilies and aromatic spices. It's bold, flavorful, and will leave you wanting more!
